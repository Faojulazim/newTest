About Me

I've been passionate about PC building and tech repairs since I was young. What started as a hobby quickly turned into something more when I realized how overpriced pre-built systems were. I wanted to offer affordable, high-quality, and customized solutions for people who need a reliable PC that fits their exact needs.

Unlike big retailers, I work closely with my customers to ensure they get a personalized experience. Whether you're new to PCs or a tech enthusiast, I'm here to provide expert guidance, honest pricing, and hands-on support for your system. Let’s build something great together!

My goal isn’t just to make money—it’s to make technology accessible and fair. I believe in honest pricing, clear communication, and giving people exactly what they need without unnecessary extras. If you're looking for a custom-built PC, an upgrade, or a small repair, I’d be happy to help!

What Makes Me Different

Unlike big retailers, I offer fully custom-built PCs—no generic pre-built systems, no one-size-fits-all solutions. I believe that every person is different, and their PC should be too. Whether you need a powerful workstation, a budget-friendly gaming setup, or something in between, I make sure that your build is tailored to your specific needs, preferences, and budget.

What also sets me apart is my direct and personal customer support. There’s no AI chatbot, no long wait times—when you have a question or an issue, you can reach out to me directly. If something isn’t working, I’ll check it out—no hassle, no unnecessary delays. I value clear and open communication, meaning you won’t have to wait three to five business days for a response. If you text me, I’ll get back to you as soon as I can.

Additionally, I make sure that:

  • Every PC is tested before it reaches the customer
  • I provide ongoing support, so you're not left figuring things out alone.
  • I take the time to understand your needs, ensuring you get the best build possible.

When you buy a PC from me, you're not just getting a product—you're getting a service built on trust, expertise, and real customer care

Warranty & Support

I offer a two-year general warranty on every PC I build, ensuring that if anything goes wrong within that period, I’ll take care of it. However, I also go beyond that— since every component comes with its own manufacturer warranty, I make sure you get the best possible coverage. If a part like your RAM, CPU, or GPU has a warranty that extends beyond my two-year coverage—such as a b five-year manufacturer warranty—I will personally check with the manufacturer or retailer to see if it can still be repaired or replaced. My commitment doesn’t stop when my own warranty ends; I will always help you navigate the warranty process, troubleshoot issues, and ensure you get the most out of your system. Whether it’s within my warranty period or covered by the manufacturer, I’ll make sure you’re not left dealing with problems alone.
